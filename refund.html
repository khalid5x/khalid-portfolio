Last updated: April 19, 2026

We are committed to delivering quality work. This policy explains your refund rights based on project stage.

1. Refund Eligibility by Stage

StageRefund

2. How to Request a Refund

  • Send a written request to khalidtrrk@gmail.com or WhatsApp +966 50 918 2112.
  • Include your order number and the reason for the request.
  • We will review the request within 3 business days and respond with a decision.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 7–14 business days, using the same payment method you originally used. Payment provider processing times may extend this window.

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Third-party costs already paid on your behalf (hosting, domain, premium plugins, stock assets, licenses).
  • Consultation calls and completed strategy sessions.
  • Rush delivery fees once expedited work has begun.
  • Projects cancelled due to client inactivity (no response for more than 30 days).

5. Dispute Resolution

If you are not satisfied with a deliverable, contact us first. In most cases we can resolve issues through additional revisions covered by your package. We aim to resolve disputes amicably before any refund or escalation.

6. Chargebacks

Please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your payment provider. Unjustified chargebacks may result in termination of services and pursuit of reimbursement through appropriate legal channels.

7. Force Majeure

We are not liable for delays or service interruptions caused by events beyond our reasonable control (natural disasters, internet outages, infrastructure failures). In such cases we will resume work promptly when possible.
