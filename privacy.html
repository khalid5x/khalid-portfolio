Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Khalid ("we", "us", "I") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit khalidtech.onrender.com or engage our freelance technology services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details you voluntarily provide: name, email address, phone/WhatsApp number.
  • Order details: service selected, package tier, payment method, and any notes you share.
  • Analytics data: anonymized visit timestamps, country, and referrer, used to understand site traffic.
  • Communications: messages you send via the contact form or WhatsApp.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your order and deliver the requested service.
  • To send invoices and payment confirmations.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To improve the website and our service offerings.

3. Payment Information

We do not store credit card or bank account numbers on our servers. All payments are processed through third-party providers (STC Pay, Apple Pay, PayPal, Wise, bank transfer). Please review their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Payment providers strictly to process your transaction.
  • Legal authorities when required by applicable law.

5. Data Retention

Order records and contact messages are retained for up to 5 years for accounting and tax compliance, then deleted. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

7. Cookies

This site uses minimal functional cookies and local storage only — no third-party advertising trackers. Service Worker caching is used to improve performance and enable offline access.

8. Security

Order invoices are protected with private access tokens. We apply reasonable technical measures to safeguard your data, but no online transmission is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date reflects the most recent revision.
